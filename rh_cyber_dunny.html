'Cyber dunny' mars streetscape

LOCAL residents who appreciate and enjoy the truly "village" atmosphere of the Blackburn shopping centre must be wondering why it is that our local town planners, councillors and the MET are so hell-bent on desecrating the streetscape in South Pde.

   The latest monstrosity foisted on us is the cyber dunny from hell that has just landed almost opposite the end of Gardenia St in what is left of our once-fine village gardens. Looking like Dr Who's Tardis, this in my view is an evil construction and an eyesore beyond compare.

   I understand that all of us need the convenience of a convenience now and again. But why, oh why, couldn't this bunker be built in the carparks behind the South Pde shops? A convenient sign could have pointed Pauline and Percy in the right direction for a comfort station - and our streetscape would not have suffered such a beastly blight.

Robin F. Howells,
Nunawading.

Whitehorse Gazette, December 1, 1999
