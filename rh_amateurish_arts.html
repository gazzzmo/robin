Amateurish arts

It was great to see the letter by Bronwen Kiely furiously plugging the new Sunday arts show. It took my breath away, as I’m sure it did for other GG readers who suffered while trying to listen to the new show.

   At least Sian Prior had class and real knowledge of the arts. I found the show more like a Chatterbox Corner than anything worth listening to.

   The untimely gaps in the presentation, the microphones left open when they should have been closed and the inane yapping all added to its extreme amateurism.

Robin F. Howells, Nunawading

The Age Green Guide - February 10, 2000
